About Me

Jack Wilderman is a UK-based wildlife photographer with a passion for capturing the raw and unfiltered beauty of the natural world. For over a decade, Jack has ventured into some of the most remote and untamed corners of the planet, documenting the wildlife that inhabits these extraordinary landscapes. Working predominantly within the Scottish wilderness his work tells the stories of creatures both great and small, capturing the fleeting moments that reveal their power, grace, and vulnerability.

Jack’s love for nature began as a child, wandering the woodlands near his home, where he developed a deep respect for the delicate balance of the wild. This early connection inspired him to pick up a camera, driven by a desire to share the incredible encounters he witnessed. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a lifelong journey to explore and document wildlife through his lens.
